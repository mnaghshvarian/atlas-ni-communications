About Us

Pioneering the Future of Optical Communications

Atlas NI Communications Corp. is a subsidiary of AI Atlas Inc., specifically dedicated to the application of Natural Intelligence (NI) technology in fiber optic communications and optical transceivers. Founded in 2025, we are pioneering software-defined transceiver solutions using Natural Intelligence (NI)—an advanced AI-inspired technology that optimizes high-speed optical and wireless communications in real-time.

In today's hyper-connected world, high-speed telecom and datacenter networks are essential for powering everything from cloud computing and artificial intelligence to global communication and financial systems. Our breakthrough technology addresses critical inefficiencies that threaten their sustainability and performance.

Our revolutionary technology represents a paradigm shift in optical communication, offering a smarter, greener, and faster solution to meet the demands of a data-driven world.

Reduced Latency
